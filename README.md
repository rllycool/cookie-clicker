# About
Auto clicker for [Cookie Clicker on Steam](https://store.steampowered.com/app/1454400/Cookie_Clicker/), using OS level redirects via Python.

![alt text](image.png)

# How to use
`locate.py`: Finds (x,y) coordinates for clicker script.

![alt text](image-1.png)

`clicker.py`: Using located coordinates, clicks your cookie.

### How should I spend sugar lumps?
1. Wizard towers to level 1

2. Temples to level 1

3. Farms to level 1

4. Banks to level 1

5. Farms to level 9 for max garden plot size

6. Cursors to level 12 for stock market office

7. Stockpile 100 lumps for sugar baking

8. From here you can either save to get cursors to level 20 for max effect of aura and luminous gloves (stay above 100 until last level where you can drop to 94) (starting like this is better for cookie count) or go straight to levelling all buildings to level 10 then do cursors to level 20 (this is better for getting achievements faster)

9. Do not, no matter how big the temptation, waste even a single one on refilling mana or Sugar Frenzy.
